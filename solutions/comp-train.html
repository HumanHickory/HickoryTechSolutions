Frequently Asked Questions

What if I don't see my topic listed? Not a problem. Contact us and we'll let you know if we can offer a course on it. If we can't we'll provide you with a list of learning resources for free!
Are these group or individual classes? Pricing is for one-on-one lessons. However, if you want to schedule a group meeting, we'd be more than happy to accomodate!
When do these classes take place? We schedule around your time. Morning or night, we will find a way to make it work.
Where do these lessons take place? As a freelance company, we do not have an office building. However, we are more than happy to come to you or to meet you in a location that you feel comfortable in
I don't know what I don't know No worries! All of our courses are the same price, so if you end up wanting to learn more about an unscheduled topic, that's absolutely fine with us! However, we may not be able to bring additional resources with us if we don't know the topics you want to cover beforehand.
Can I purchase private lessons for my child? Of course! We are more than happy to do private lessons with the younger generation, including programming. As long as an adult (18 or older) is present, we are more than happy to schedule a lesson.
