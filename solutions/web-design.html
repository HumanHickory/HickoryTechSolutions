About our Pricing

Why are our prices so cheap? As a freelance organization, we have little overhead to account for. Hickory Tech Solutions and our partners believe that integrating technology should not cost an arm and a leg.
What if I don't like the website? The first page we build will be your home page. This page will set the theme for your entire website. If you don't like the home page, and we can't fix it, then you don't pay. Simple as that.
Are cheap prices a reflection of quality? Absolutely not. We strive to provide the highest quality of service possible. Again, as a freelance agency, we do not need to charge a high price. So we simply don't!
Why do prices say "starting price"? Starting prices assume you already have the content typed out, you just need the website built. However, since we offer so many add-on services, it is impossible for us to calculate an accurate price range.
I have no idea where to start or what I need. Not a problem! Simply send us an email or give us a call and we'd be more than happy to walk you through your new website, every step of the way.
